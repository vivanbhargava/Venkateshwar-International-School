Welcome To Venkateshwar International School

...Three words that spell magic in the field of education
Venkateshwar International School...

Established by the Sri Venkateshwar Education Society (Regd.) in the year 2001, Venkateshwar International School is a premier world-class school located in a serene and sprawling four acre campus in Sector 10, Dwarka, New Delhi.

Voted as one amongst the seven architectural marvels of West Delhi, the V.I.S. building stands tall and magnificent in its majestic splendour, not just in physical terms, but also, in its undeniable formidable reputation for providing quality education to its students. The excellent school infrastructure is accentuated by beautiful landscaping, manicured lawns and a well-maintained games and sports astro-turf field.
V.I.S. is an English medium, co-educational, CBSE affiliated school having eminent educationists, bureaucrats, technocrats, intellectuals and prominent professionals as members of the Management Committee and the Advisory Board.

News & Events

  • Class XII Virtual Orientation Session

    As rightly said, "Education is the key to unlocking the world, a passport to freedom."

    The newly promoted Class XII students of VIS attended an orientation session conducted by Ms. Jyotsna Bharadwaj. The session was titled Navigating Through Class XII Happily & Effectively. It served as food for thought for all the students. The salient points of discussion were stress & time-management, problems encountered while studying effectively, varied learning styles & methodologies, factors affecting academics, smart skills, memorization techniques many such skills which espouse efficient studying methods. The students participated enthusiastically in the session and were extremely benefited by this webinar.

  • VIS student wins at National Level

    We feel proud to share that Guransh Gambhir from class 2 has won National Champion Trophy & has secured National rank 5 in the 6th International Abacus Olympiad.
    He has also won a Gold medal in 6th International Online Abacus Competition BRAINOBRAINFEST 2021
    We wish him luck.

  • Online interaction with students in Korea

    VIS students of class XII had a session with students at Ulsan Foreign Language High School, Korea. The Korean students were at their school while the VIS students were at home. It was an interesting discussion on multiple topics ranging from studies to career options attended by about 50 members . The School head Dr. Manisha Sharma also attended the session to know about strategies employed by Korean schools to cope up with lockdown . The Korean students got to know a lot about India’s Lockdown and economy through presentations by Meyhar Dudeja and Ravneet Kaur of class XII . This was the first such webinar in this series .

  • Class 3 Virtual Orientation

    New session, new beginnings……

    To apprise the parents with the system and transitions in class III, an Orientation Session was held on 10th April,2021. To begin with, our honorable principal, Dr. Manisha Sharma addressed the parents and talked about the ethos and culture of VIS. The headmistress, Primary Wing, Ms. Anuradha Kumar further gave an insight of the curricular and co- curricular framework. She also emphasized on the activity -based online teaching being followed by the dedicated teachers to work for the holistic development of every child. The parents were happy to be a part of the session and their queries were resolved. The session concluded with a formal introduction of the class teachers, subject teachers and activity teachers.

  • Webinar on resurgence of Covid-19

    Scientia Society of the school organised an informative session on the second wave of Covid 19 which has taken the entire country by storm. The session was organised for the students of classes VI-XII. The session focussed on how the current wave is different from the first one in symptoms and spread, and what measures can be taken to contain this deadly trend.
    Students were acquainted with the concept of immunization and the ways to boost their immunity.
    Interactive myth or fact session busted the misconceptions people have regarding immunization and the spread of this disease. The importance of mental health during these difficult times was also stressed upon and students were given tips on how to engage themselves constructively in these unprecedented times.
